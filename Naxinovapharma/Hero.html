About NexiNova Pharma

Nexinova Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. Established on 24 October 2025


Nexinova Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. is an emerging healthcare company dedicated to advancing women’s health and delivering trusted multispeciality pharmaceutical solutions. We are committed to providing high-quality, research-driven medicines designed to improve patient outcomes, support healthcare professionals, and enhance quality of life at every stage.



Our core strength lies in our scientific approach, industry expertise, and a passion for innovation in gynecology and multispeciality therapeutic areas. Nexinova aims to become one of the most reliable and respected pharmaceutical brands in India and beyond.



We work closely with healthcare professionals to understand patient needs and bring innovative, cost-effective therapies to the market. Nexinova is driven by a team of passionate individuals with extensive experience in pharma marketing, research, and product development.
